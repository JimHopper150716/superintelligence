Aditya's Personal Blog

March 28th 2036

A month has gone by. I know I have been documenting in this blog less and less, but I thought that was what I wanted when I got Katherine. I think I am just seeking solitude here. A moment of peace from my own thinking, by just documenting it. I write because I must, otherwise I will go crazy. I think I am going crazy. Or I already am. Katherine seems different lately. She says it's nothing. or my own voice says it's nothing. I can't distinguish between my own thoughts and Katherine these days, so I can't experience if I do have any company with me, let alone a companion. I have begun hallucinating in this torture chamber. Yesterday, I woke up to an alarm in the kitchen station, when I went there, I thought the microwave had caught fire, but I soon came back to reality. There was nothing. I have been imagining the alarms quite frequently. Late at nights, when I'm sleeping I wake up to these buzzing, jarring sounds, only to find out there's nothing. Katherine is connected to the computer system of the entire satellite station; I ask her to run routine troubleshooting checks almost every day, but she says it's nothing. Or I say, it's nothing, I can't tell.
