Delivery

January 8 2036 | #feelsweird

Bought the subscription. Took a substantial chunk of my salary to do so. Feels weird you know. The delivery of the model is today. I'll just get a notification on the station computer. And then boom, it's installed. I mean there is some anxiety as well, I don't know how the training turned out to be after I left. And how they commercialized it. I hear the same system is aiding government offices in 20-something countries now. I cannot make you understand. I cannot make anyone understand what is happening inside me. I cannot even explain it to myself. I write differently from what I speak, I speak differently from what I think, I think differently from the way I ought to think, and so it all proceeds into deepest darkness. All language is but a poor translation.
