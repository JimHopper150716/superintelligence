Hopes for The Future

A letter to Myself

I wrote this letter on the takeaway bag when our department head took us to the McDonald's near the campus to celebrate. Although there were some ecstatic celebration inside me for a few moments, it quickly died down. We were working on this for so long, we had finally did it. But soon the realization came: should have we? I was highly paranoid. I am. But I have good reason to be. I can't stop it now. It's already out there. I just hope I did not contribute, no matter how small, to something actualizes the threat to our existence.
